Kelenken "Winged Deity"
"kell-en-ken"

Length: 4.3m
Height: 3m
Area: South America
Climate: Humid
Period: Mid Miocene

It is known for it's large sharp beak, 
talons, and strong neck. It was very 
agile and fast, being able to reach 
quicker speeds than horses. It's also 
by far the biggest "Terror Bird"

Kelenken is a part of the 
Phorusrhacidae family.

Dodo "Plump and Lovable"
"dough-dough"

Length: 1m
Height: 1m
Area: Island of Maruitius
Climate: Tropical
Period: Holocene
Family: Columbidae

Dodo birds were herbivores, feeding 
on nuts, seeds, bulbs, roots, and 
fallen fruit.

It is known as a bird with no fear by how 
unflinching they appeared around humans. 
Dodos were driven to extinction by the 
men who populated the island.

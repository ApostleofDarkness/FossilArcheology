Brachiosaurus "Arm Lizard"
"brack-e-o-sawr-us"

Length: 25-26m
Height: 5-7m
Area: North America and Africa
Climate:  Semi-arid
Period: Jurassic
Family: Brachiosauridae

Brachiosaurus were herbivores, feeding 
on foliage well above the ground.

It was considered one of the largest of 
dinosaurs but this title is currently 
taken by Argentinosaurus.

Allosaurus "Other Lizard"
"Al-owe-sore-uss"

Length: 8.5m
Height: 3m
Area: North America and Europe
Climate: Dry
Period: Late Jurassic
Family: Allosauridae

Allosaurus were carnivores, feeding on
 large herbivores such as the Stegosaurus.

As an iconic predatore of the Jurassic, 
it is known for its distinct eyebrow 
ridges and large claws.

Pachycephalosaurus "Thick Headed Lizard"
"pack-ee-seph-uh-loh-sawr-us"

Length: 4-5m
Height: 4.3m
Area: North America
Climate: Tropical
Period: Cretaceous
Family: Pachycephalosauridae

Pachycephalosaurus were herbivores,
feeding on leaves, seeds, fruit, and 
insects but not vegetation fed upon 
large herbivores due to its small, 
ridged teeth.

It is distrinct for its thick,large skull 
in comparison to Pachycephalosaurids.

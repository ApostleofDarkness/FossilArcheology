Spinosaurus "Spine Lizard"
"spine-o-sawr-us"

Length: 12-18m
Height: 5m
Area: North Africa
Climate: Tropical
Period: Cretaceous
Family: Spinosauridae

Spinosaurus were mainly piscivorous,
feeding on fish and occasionally 
other dinosaurs.

It is distinct for its crocodillian 
jaws, large spines, and for being the 
largest carnivorous dinosaur ever.

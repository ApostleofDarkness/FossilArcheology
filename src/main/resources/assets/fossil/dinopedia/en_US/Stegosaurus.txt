Stegosaurus "Roof Lizard"
"steg-o-sawr-us"

Length: 9m
Height: 4m
Area: North America and Europe (Portugal)
Climate: Semi-Arid
Period: Jurassic
Family: Stegosauridae

Stegosaurus were herbivores, feeding 
on low vegetation.

Its body has large plates on its body 
and large spikes called a thagomizer 
on its tail.

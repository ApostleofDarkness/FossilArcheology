Quagga "Any Zebra"
"kwag-uh"

Length: 257cm
Height: 125-135cm
Area: South Africa
Climate: Arid
Period: Holocene
Family: Equidae

Quaggas were herbivores, feeding on 
grass and shrubs.

It is known for it's distinct patterns from 
the head to the lower body. It was driven 
to extinction by man, and has spurred
projects aiming to recreate the creature.

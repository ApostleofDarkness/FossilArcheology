Mosasaurus "Lizard of the Meuse River"
"moe-za-sawr-us"

Length: 15-18m
Height: 1m
Area: Western Europe and North America
Climate: Tropical*
Period: Cretaceous
Family: Mosasauridae

Mosasaurus were carnivores, feeding 
on other marine reptiles.

Mosasaurus is longer and more robust 
than its tylosaurine relatives.

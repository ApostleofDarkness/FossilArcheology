Compsognathus "Elegant Jaw"
"komp-sog-nay-thuss"

Length: 1m
Height: 0.6m
Area: Europe (France and Germany)
Climate: Tropical
Period: Jurassic
Family: Compsognathinae

Compsognathus were carnivores, feeding 
on small vertibrates such as lizards.

It is one of the few dinosaurs with a 
diet known for certainy as remains of 
small lizards were preserved in the 
bellies of some specimens.

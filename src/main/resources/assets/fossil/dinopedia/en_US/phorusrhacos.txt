Phorusrhacos "Rag Thief"
"FOR-rus-RAH-kos"

Length: 3.9m 
Height: 2.4m 
Area: South America 
Climate: Tropical 
Period: Early Miocene - Mid Miocene

It is known for it's large sharp 
beak, talons, and strong neck. 
It was very agile and fast,
being able to reach quicker 
speeds than horses.

Phorusrhacos is a part of
 the Phorusrhacidae family.

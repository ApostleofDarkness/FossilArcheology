Plesiosaur (Plesiosaurus) "Near Lizard"
"pleh-see-oh-sawr" (pleh-see-oh-sawr-us)

Length: 4m
Height: 1m
Area: Every continent including Antartica
Climate: Varies within species
Period: Triassic - Creatceous
Family:

Plesiosaurs were piscivores, feeding 
on fish and squid.

It is distinct for its manueverable 
form built not for speed.

Plesiosaur is the order name which 
includes suborders Plesiosauroidea 
and Pliosauroidea.

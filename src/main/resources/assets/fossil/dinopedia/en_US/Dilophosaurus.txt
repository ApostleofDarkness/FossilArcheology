Dilophosaurus "Two Crested Lizard"
"die-lo-foh-sawr-us"

Length; 6 m
Height: 1.5-2m
Area: North America
Climate: Arid to Semi-Arid
Period: Jurassic
Family: Dilophosauridae

Dilophosaurus were carnivores, having 
fed on herbivorous dinosaurs, such as 
prosauropods and Scutellosaurus.

It is notable for the double 
crests on it's skull.

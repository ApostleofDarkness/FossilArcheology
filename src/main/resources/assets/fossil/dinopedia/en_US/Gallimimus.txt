Gallimimus "Chicken Mimic"
"gall-e-mime-uss"

Length: 8m
Height: 2m
Area: Asia
Climate: Arid
Period: Cretaceous
Family: Ornithomimidae

Gallimimus were omnivores, feeding 
on aquatic invertibrates, insects, 
and plants.

It is distinct from other Ornithomimds 
with its exceptionally elongated skull.

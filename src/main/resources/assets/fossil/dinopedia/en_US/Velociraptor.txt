Velociraptor "Speedy Thief"
"vel-oss-ih-rap-tor"

Length: 1.5-2m
Height: 46-70cm
Area: Asia (China)
Climate: Arid
Period: Cretaceous
Family: Dromaeosauridae

Velociraptors were carnivores, feeding 
on small herbivorous dinosaurs such 
as the Protoceratops.

It is a fiercely intelligent creature 
with a ferocious appetite. It is 
believed to have hunted in packs to 
take down large prey.

Gastornis "Gaston's Bird" 
"gas-tore-nis"

Lenght: 2m
Height: 2.2m
Area: Europe and Norht America
Climate: Marine
Period: Late Paleocene - Late Eocene

It is known for it's large sharp beak, 
talons, and strong neck. It was very 
agile and fast, being able to reach 
quicker speeds than horses. It is 
also the only "Terror Bird" that 
ate plants as well as meat.

Gastornis is a part of the 
Phorusrhacidae family.

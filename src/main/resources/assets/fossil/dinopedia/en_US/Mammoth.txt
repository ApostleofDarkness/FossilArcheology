Mammoth (Wooly Mammoth) 
"Firstborn Elephant"
"mam-uhth" (woo-ly mam-uhth)

Length: 5-7.5m
Height: 2.7-3.4m
Area: North America, Asia, Europe, Russia
Climate: Subartic
Period: Pleistocene
Family: Elephantidae

Wooly mammoths were herbivores, feeding 
on shrubs, mosses, flowering plants, 
tree matter, etc.

It is known from various specimen found 
in areas like Siberia, having bodies 
preserved enough to bleed even.

Smilodon "Knife Tooth"
"smile-oh-don"

Length: 210cm
Height: 100cm
Area: North America, Mexico, Africa, 
      Europe, and South America
Climate: Arid
Period: Pleistocene
Family: Felidae

Smilodons were carnivores, feeding on large 
herbivores, such as bison or camels.

It is distinct for it's large dagger like 
teeth and it's robust build in comparison 
to other big cats of the modern day.

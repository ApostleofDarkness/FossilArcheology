Coelacanth "Hollow Spine"
"see-la-kanth"

Length: .2m
Height: .08m
Area: African Coasts/Islands near Australia
Climate: Tropical
Period: Devonian - Recent
Order: Coelacanth

Coelacanths are carnivores, believed to 
consume squid, eels, small sharks, etc.

It is known as a living fossil, having 
survived, similiar to the Nautilus, 
millions of years since extinction of 
the dinosaurs.

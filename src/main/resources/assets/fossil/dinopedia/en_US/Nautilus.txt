Nautilus "Sailor"
"nawt-i-luhs"

Length: 20cm
Height: 1m
Area: Indo-Pacific
Climate: Tropical*
Period: Triassic - Present
Family: Nautilidae

Nautilus is a scavenger and opportunistic 
predators, eating molts of lobsters, hermit 
crabs, and carrion.

It is known as represents the only living 
member of it's family, having remained 
unchanged for millions of years.
